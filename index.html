About Us

We are the student council of 21K school and we are here to help you with any problem you have! We always strive to organize fun events so that your school life is fun! We love to come up with creative ideas and help people around us! We made this website so you can contact us if you need any help. You can also see all the upcoming events and you might also be lucky to see your photo in this website!
